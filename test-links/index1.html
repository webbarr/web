Hi! I’m Web

Design/Code/Strategy

Born William Edward Barr and subsequently nicknamed “Web” by my parents, I’ve always been into design, media and awesome products. Currently, I’m a digital producer for National Geographic magazine focusing on telling great stories and improving our user’s experience. I’m also the creator of NatGeo Found, the official Tumblr for National Geographic which gives people a look into the National Geographic archives.


If you need help building your website or want to chat about improving your digital prescence, I’m currently available for user experience design consulting; as well as, social marketing and content strategy.


You can find me speaking my mind on Twitter and sharing moments on Instagram. But if you'd like to talk more about anything from your next big idea—to new digital editorial strategies—to European soccer, drop me a line below!
